A Goal

12 December at 8:30PM

I’m always far behind on listening to music—at the time I write this, I have 8523 tracks that haven’t been played. This situation only ever seems to get worse, but I haven’t been able to come up with a reasonable and attainable solution. I think I may have come up with just such a thing yesterday, though.

I’ve called my solution (more of a goal, really) ‘1000’ because of what it entails: playing a 1000 tracks each week, 250 of which have not been previously played (either number can be pushed higher.) Of course, even if I were to add no new tracks and to hold at the minimum rate, I’d be at this for thirty-four weeks.

Given that I decided on this policy part-way through Monday, I’m at something of a disadvantage; I’ve thus far played 188 songs, 116 of which are new.
